# Privacy notice template

This is **not legal advice** — it's a starting point you can adapt for the
public-facing privacy notice of the deployment. For Spain specifically,
obligations stem from the **GDPR** (Reg. (EU) 2016/679), the **LOPDGDD**
(LO 3/2018) and the **LSSI-CE** (Ley 34/2002).

> The backend stores raw IP addresses for abuse prevention. Under GDPR an
> IP is personal data, so you need a lawful basis (legitimate interest
> works here), a retention policy, a way for users to exercise their
> rights, and a privacy notice.

Swap the bracketed placeholders with your own details.

---

## Privacy notice — [Quizzy Online]

Last updated: [YYYY-MM-DD]

### Who we are

[Full name / entity] (the "data controller"), reachable at
[contact email].

### What we collect when you comment or flag a question

When you submit a comment or flag through the site, we store:

- The **text** of your comment or flag reason.
- The **optional nickname** you provide (not verified, may be empty).
- The **question context** you were looking at (set identifier, question
  index, the question text and options shown to you).
- Your **IP address** and your browser's **user-agent** string, automatically
  captured when the form is submitted.
- The **timestamp** of the submission.

We do not require an account and we do not use third-party analytics or
advertising cookies.

### Why we process this data (lawful basis)

- **Comment/flag content + question context:** legitimate interest
  (Art. 6.1.f GDPR) — to improve the accuracy of the question bank and
  surface feedback to other users.
- **IP address + user-agent:** legitimate interest — to detect and mitigate
  spam, abuse and attacks, as is standard for any user-submitted-content
  service.

### Anti-spam: Cloudflare Turnstile

Every submission is verified with Cloudflare Turnstile (a CAPTCHA
alternative). Turnstile may set cookies and analyze browser signals to
distinguish bots from humans. See Cloudflare's privacy policy at
https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/.

### Retention

- **Content** (comment/flag text, question context, nickname, timestamp) is
  kept for as long as the submission remains relevant to the moderation of
  the question bank. Submissions marked as spam are deleted automatically
  after [30] days.
- **IP address and user-agent** are redacted automatically after [180]
  days.

You can request earlier deletion (see **Your rights** below).

### Sharing

We do not sell or share comment data with third parties. The data lives
in Cloudflare's infrastructure (Pages Functions + D1); see Cloudflare's
data-protection documentation.

### Your rights

You can exercise the following rights under GDPR by writing to
[contact email]:

- Access — ask what data we hold about your submissions.
- Rectification — fix inaccurate data.
- Erasure — ask us to delete your comments and associated data.
- Restriction and objection to processing.
- Lodge a complaint with the Spanish data-protection authority (AEPD,
  https://www.aepd.es).

Because submissions are anonymous we may need information from you to
locate them (e.g. an approximate date, the content of the comment).

### Changes

If we change this notice we'll update the "Last updated" date above and,
for material changes, notify users on the site.
